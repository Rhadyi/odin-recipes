Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for making lasagna a very tasty dish

The dish is very common in north america

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Olive Oil
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Beat eggs
  2. Mix with water
  3. Add in salt
  4. Pound flour
  5. Mix all together
  6. Add in the oven
