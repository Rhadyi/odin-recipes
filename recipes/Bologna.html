Bologna

Description

This is a recipe for making Bologna,a dish that is made mostly of meat

It is usually served as a combination of different dishes and/or menu

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Liquid smoke flavoring
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix groud beef with salt, garlic powder, onion powder and liquid smoke
  2. Divide in half and form into two rolls
  3. Wrap with a plastic wrap
  4. Pre heat the oven
  5. Add the mixture to the oven for 15 minutes
