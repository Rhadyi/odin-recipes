Noodles

Description

This is a recipe for healthy egg noodles.

It originated from China and Vietnam but has enjoyed a lot of popularity in the western part of the world

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs beaten
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Beat eggs
  2. Mix the eggs with milk, butter, salt and the flour to form a dough
  3. Knead dough till it's smooth
  4. Let the dough rest in a covered space for 7 minutes
  5. Roll and cut dough into 8 equal parts
  6. Air dry the mixture
  7. Boil the mixture in salted water for 30 minutes
